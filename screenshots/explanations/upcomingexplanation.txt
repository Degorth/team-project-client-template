upcomingscrn:
This is the page that shows all of the events that will be coming up in the near
future. It will show the activity name, the organizer and the picture associated
with that club or even and the time/date when this will occur. Furthermore, it
will contain a rating based on either the user's preference or the average
rating of the other users who have attended this event in the past. There will
also be a short description which will end with a link to the event's page. The
columns having to do with organizer, time, date, and rating will be sortable.
